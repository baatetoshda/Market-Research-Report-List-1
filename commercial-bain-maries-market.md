<p><ul><li>Wet Heat Bain Maries</li><li>Dry Heat Bain Maries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial bain marie market is segmented into wet heat and dry heat bain maries. Wet heat bain maries use water to maintain temperature, ideal for keeping dishes warm without direct heat, ensuring even cooking and moisture retention. Conversely, dry heat bain maries rely on heat conduction without water, providing a more direct warming method suitable for foods that should not contain excess moisture. Both types are essential in foodservice environments for efficient food handling and preservation.</p></p>
