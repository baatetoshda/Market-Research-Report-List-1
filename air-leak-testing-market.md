<p><p>The air leak testing market comprises various methods, each suited for specific applications. Pressure decay testing detects leaks by monitoring pressure changes in a sealed environment. Helium sniff testing utilizes helium gas, which is sensitive and can identify small leaks by sniffing around potential breach points. Nitrogen purge testing involves purging a system with nitrogen to highlight defects. Other methods may include bubble testing or electrical resistance techniques, offering diverse solutions for maintaining product integrity and safety across industries.</p></p>
