<p><ul><li>Flame Spraying</li><li>Arc Spraying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal sprayed aluminum coatings market includes various application methods, primarily flame spraying and arc spraying. Flame spraying involves melting aluminum wire or powder and projecting it onto a substrate using a flame, creating a robust protective layer. In contrast, arc spraying employs an electric arc to melt the aluminum, which is then atomized and sprayed onto the surface. Both techniques provide corrosion resistance and thermal protection, making them valuable for industries like aerospace, marine, and construction.</p></p>
