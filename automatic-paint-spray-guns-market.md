<p><p>The automatic paint spray guns market is primarily divided into two main types: pneumatic spray guns and electric spray guns. Pneumatic spray guns utilize compressed air to atomize paint, providing a smooth finish and efficient application, ideal for large-scale projects. In contrast, electric spray guns use electric power for paint application, offering portability and ease of use, particularly for smaller tasks. Both types are designed to enhance efficiency and precision in painting, catering to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
