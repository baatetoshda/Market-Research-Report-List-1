<p><p>The semiconductor ceramic capillary market is characterized by two primary types: Zirconia Toughened Alumina and 99.99% Alumina. Zirconia Toughened Alumina combines the strength of zirconia with the lightweight properties of alumina, enhancing mechanical robustness and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding semiconductor applications. In contrast, 99.99% Alumina offers exceptional purity and dielectric properties, making it ideal for applications requiring high electrical insulation and minimal contamination. Both materials cater to different needs within the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
