<p><p>A Direct Acting Pressure Gauge is a mechanical device used for measuring fluid pressure in various applications. It operates based on the elastic deformation of a sensing element, typically a bourdon tube, which translates pressure changes into a readable measurement on a dial. These gauges are favored for their simplicity, reliability, and ease of use in industries such as oil and gas, manufacturing, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The Direct Acting Pressure Gauge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. The trend towards automation in various sectors is also fueling the market, as more companies seek integrated solutions for monitoring and controlling pressure.
