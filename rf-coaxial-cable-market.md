<p><p>The RF coaxial cable market consists of various impedance types: 50Ω, 75Ω, 93Ω, and 95Ω. Each type is designed for specific applications; 50Ω cables are typically used in radio frequency transmission, providing lower loss and higher power handling, making them ideal for communication systems. In contrast, 75Ω cables excel in video and broadcast applications, offering minimal signal reflection. The 93Ω and 95Ω cables serve niche applications, with 93Ω often used in laboratory settings, while 95Ω is less common but valuable for specialized uses.</p></p>
