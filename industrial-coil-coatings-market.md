<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Back Paint</li><li>Topcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial coil coatings market consists of three key segments: primer, back paint, and topcoat. Primer serves as an initial layer to enhance adhesion and protect the substrate from corrosion. Back paint, typically applied to the reverse side of coils, safeguards against environmental factors and provides aesthetic appeal. The topcoat is the final, visible layer that offers additional protection, color, and finish, contributing to durability and long-term performance in various industrial applications. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring product longevity and reliability.</p></p>
