<p><p>Blank printed circuit board (PCB) labels are essential for identifying and tracking components on PCBs. There are primarily two market types: coated paper labels and polyimide (PI) labels. Coated paper labels are cost-effective and suitable for short-term use, typically in less demanding environments. In contrast, polyimide labels are more durable, heat-resistant, and suitable for high-temperature applications, making them ideal for long-term identification in aerospace and automotive sectors. Both types cater to different labeling needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
