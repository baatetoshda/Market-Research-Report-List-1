<p><p>The plantar fasciitis splint market is primarily divided into two types: dorsal and traditional splints. Dorsal splints are designed to exert gentle traction on the foot, providing support while allowing for more natural foot movement during sleep. They are typically less bulky and more comfortable. In contrast, traditional splints often feature a more rigid construction that holds the foot at a fixed angle, providing substantial arch support and effectively stretching the plantar fascia. Both types aim to alleviate pain and promote healing.</p></p>
