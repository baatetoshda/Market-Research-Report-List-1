<p><p>The cardiac function non-invasive testing analyzer market includes several types of technologies designed to assess heart performance without invasive procedures. Electrical Impedance Cardiography (EIC) measures changes in electrical impedance through the thorax to gauge cardiac output and other hemodynamic parameters. Echocardiography utilizes sound waves to create images of the heart, enabling assessment of structural and functional aspects. Other methods may encompass techniques like photoplethysmography and magnetic resonance imaging, offering diverse approaches for evaluating cardiac health and function.</p></p>
