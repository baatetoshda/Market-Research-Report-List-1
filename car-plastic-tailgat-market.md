<p><p>The car plastic tailgate market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and thermoset tailgates. Thermoplastic tailgates are made from polymers that can be reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and recyclability, which enhances their appeal in manufacturing. Conversely, thermoset tailgates are composed of polymers that undergo a chemical change when cured, resulting in a durable and heat-resistant product, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types cater to varying automotive needs, balancing performance, cost, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
