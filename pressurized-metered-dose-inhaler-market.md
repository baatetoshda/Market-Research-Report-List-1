<p><p>The Pressurized Metered-Dose Inhaler (pMDI) market consists of two primary types: single-dose inhalers and multi-dose inhalers. Single-dose inhalers dispense one dose per actuation, ensuring precision and minimizing waste, making them ideal for acute conditions. Multi-dose inhalers, on the other hand, can deliver several doses from the same device, offering convenience and suitability for chronic conditions that require ongoing treatment. Both types cater to diverse patient needs in respiratory therapy, enhancing medication delivery efficiency.</p></p>
