<p><strong>The Atrial Fibrillation (AF) Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrocardiogram (ECG)</li><li>Echocardiogram (echo)</li><li>Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE)</li><li>Chest X-ray</li><li>Blood Tests</li><li>Stress Testing</li><li>Electrical Activity Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Atrial Fibrillation diagnostics market comprises several key types of tests. Electrocardiograms (ECG) record heart electrical activity to identify irregular rhythms. Echocardiograms (echo) use ultrasound to visualize heart structures and assess function. Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE) provides detailed images by inserting a probe in the esophagus. Chest X-rays help detect heart enlargement or fluid. Blood tests can indicate underlying issues, while stress testing evaluates heart performance under physical strain. Electrical activity testing further assesses heart rhythm abnormalities.</p></p>
