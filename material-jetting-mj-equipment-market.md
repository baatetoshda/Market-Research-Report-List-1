<p><p>The Material Jetting (MJ) equipment market encompasses various technologies, including Ink Jetting, Binder Jetting, and Aerosol Jetting. Ink Jetting uses liquid materials selectively deposited to create fine details in 3D objects, ideal for high-resolution prints. Binder Jetting involves applying a binding agent to layer powders, fostering solid structures efficiently. Aerosol Jetting, on the other hand, employs aerosolized droplets for precise material deposition, enabling a diverse range of materials and applications. Each type offers unique advantages for additive manufacturing.</p></p>
