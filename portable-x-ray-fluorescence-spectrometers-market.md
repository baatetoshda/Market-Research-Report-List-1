<p><strong>The Portable X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersion</li><li>Energy Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) Spectrometers Market is segmented into two main types: Wavelength Dispersion and Energy Dispersion. Wavelength Dispersion XRF spectrometers utilize diffraction gratings to separate emitted X-rays based on their wavelengths, providing high-resolution elemental analysis. In contrast, Energy Dispersion XRF spectrometers employ semiconductor detectors to measure the energy of the emitted X-rays directly, allowing for faster, real-time analysis. Each type has unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in fields like archaeology, environmental monitoring, and material science.</p></p>
