<p><p>The cosmetic grade zinc oxide market primarily comprises two types: indirect process zinc oxide and nano zinc oxide. Indirect process zinc oxide is produced through the oxidation of zinc metal, resulting in a larger particle size that is suitable for various cosmetic applications, such as sunscreens and makeup. On the other hand, nano zinc oxide features much smaller particle sizes, allowing for better skin absorption and transparency in formulations. Both types are valued for their UV protection, stability, and safety in cosmetic products.</p></p>
