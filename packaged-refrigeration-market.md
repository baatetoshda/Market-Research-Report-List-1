<p><p>The packaged refrigeration market features various systems, including the double pipe and three pipe systems. The double pipe system utilizes two pipes—one for the refrigerant and another for the secondary fluid—allowing for efficient heat exchange. In contrast, the three pipe system integrates an additional pipe for returning the refrigerant, enabling simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones. Both systems cater to specific industrial and commercial refrigeration needs, enhancing energy efficiency and operational flexibility.</p></p>
