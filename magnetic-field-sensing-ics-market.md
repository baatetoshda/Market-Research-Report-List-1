<p><p>The magnetic field sensing IC market comprises several technologies, including Hall Effect, Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR), Anisotropic Magnetoresistance (AMR), and Giant Magnetoresistance (GMR) sensing ICs. Hall Effect sensors detect magnetic fields using voltage changes, while TMR sensors leverage quantum mechanical effects for high sensitivity. AMR sensors operate based on changes in electrical resistance with magnetic field orientation, and GMR sensors provide enhanced sensitivity by exploiting multiple magnetic layers. Each type serves diverse applications across automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
