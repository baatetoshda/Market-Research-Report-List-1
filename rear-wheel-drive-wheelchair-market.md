<p><p>The rear-wheel drive wheelchair market can be categorized into two main types based on wheel configuration: three-round and four-round wheelchairs. Three-round wheelchairs feature two large rear wheels and a single front wheel, offering enhanced maneuverability and agility, making them suitable for tight spaces. In contrast, four-round wheelchairs have two large rear wheels and two front wheels, providing greater stability and smoother rides on uneven terrain. These variations cater to different user needs, preferences, and environments.</p></p>
