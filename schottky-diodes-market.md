<p><strong>The Schottky Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Technology</li><li>Surface Mount Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Schottky diodes market is categorized into two primary types: Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT involves inserting diodes into drilled holes on circuit boards, providing robust connections suitable for high-power applications. Conversely, SMT involves mounting diodes directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, enabling compact designs and higher assembly speeds. Each technology offers unique advantages, with THT favored for durability and SMT preferred for efficiency and miniaturization in modern electronic devices.</p></p>
