<p><p>Cesium Iodide (CsI) is utilized in various applications based on its crystal types. CsI(Tl) is doped with thallium and is known for its high-efficiency scintillation properties, making it ideal for radiation detection. CsI(Na) is doped with sodium, offering good light output and fast response times, commonly used in medical imaging. Pure CsI is utilized in specialized applications where impurities could affect performance, such as in certain optical devices and research settings, ensuring high purity and consistency.</p></p>
