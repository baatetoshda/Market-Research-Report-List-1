<p><p>The pulp and paper chemical market consists of three key types: functional chemicals, bleaching chemicals, and process chemicals. Functional chemicals enhance the properties of the final paper product, including strength, brightness, and printability. Bleaching chemicals are essential for achieving desired whiteness and removing impurities from pulp, enabling higher quality paper. Process chemicals facilitate various stages of paper manufacturing, such as pulping, refining, and coating, improving efficiency and product consistency. Together, these chemicals play a vital role in the industry’s performance and sustainability.</p></p>
