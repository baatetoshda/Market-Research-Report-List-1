<p><p>The fire-resistant tape market consists of two main types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features an adhesive on one side, making it ideal for securing materials or sealing joints where only one surface is accessible. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for stronger bonds between two surfaces, making it suitable for applications requiring more robust adhesion, such as insulation and construction projects. Both types are essential for enhancing safety and durability in various industries.</p></p>
