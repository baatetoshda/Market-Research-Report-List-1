<p><strong>The Dental CAD/CAM Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Milling Machines</li><li>Furnaces</li><li>Integrated Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental CAD/CAM technology encompasses three primary market types: milling machines, furnaces, and integrated systems. Milling machines are used to precisely shape dental restorations from materials like zirconia or resin. Furnaces are essential for processing and sintering these materials, ensuring durability and optimal properties for dental applications. Integrated systems combine both milling and furnace functionalities, streamlining workflows in dental labs by offering a cohesive solution for designing and fabricating dental prosthetics efficiently, ultimately enhancing productivity and precision in dental practices.</p></p>
