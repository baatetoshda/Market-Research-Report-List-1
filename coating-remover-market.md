<p><ul><li>The Caustic Type</li><li>The Acidic Type</li><li>The Solvent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating remover market comprises three primary types: caustic, acidic, and solvent. Caustic type removers harness strong alkaline substances to break down coatings effectively, suitable for tough, heavy-duty applications. Acidic type removers rely on acidic solutions that can dissolve rust and corrosion, often used for metal surfaces. Solvent type removers utilize organic solvents to dissolve various coatings, providing a more gentle option for intricate surfaces. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing efficacy and surface compatibility.</p></p>
