<p><p>The aerospace life jacket market consists of three main types: foam jackets, inflatable jackets, and hybrid jackets. Foam jackets offer inherent buoyancy and are rugged, making them durable for various conditions. Inflatable jackets, on the other hand, are compact and lightweight, deploying automatically when submerged, providing enhanced comfort and mobility. Hybrid jackets combine both technologies, offering the buoyancy of foam with the portability of inflatable designs, catering to diverse user needs in aviation safety systems.</p></p>
