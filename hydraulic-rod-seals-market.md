<p><strong>The Hydraulic Rod Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic rod seals market is categorized into single-acting and double-acting types. Single-acting seals are designed to facilitate motion in one direction, providing sealing effectiveness when pressure is applied to one side, making them ideal for applications like lifting and lowering. In contrast, double-acting seals support bidirectional movement, ensuring sealing integrity during both upward and downward motion, which is essential in systems requiring flexibility, such as hydraulic cylinders in industrial machinery. Both types enhance equipment efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
