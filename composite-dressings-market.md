<p><p>The composite dressings market includes several types that cater to various wound management needs. Alginate dressings are derived from seaweed, effectively absorbing exudate and promoting healing. Absorbent dressings manage fluid levels in wounds, minimizing infection risks. Anti-microbial dressings contain agents to prevent infections, particularly in chronic wounds. Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC) dressings offer moisture retention and promote healing. Silver dressings utilize antimicrobial properties of silver to combat bacteria, making them suitable for infected or high-risk wounds. Each type targets specific wound care requirements.</p></p>
