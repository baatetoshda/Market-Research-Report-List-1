<p><p>The Silicon on Insulator (SOI) wafer market is primarily divided into two types: thick SOI wafers and thin SOI wafers. Thick SOI wafers, typically over 1 micrometer, are used in high-performance applications like RF devices and advanced integrated circuits, providing excellent isolation and thermal stability. In contrast, thin SOI wafers, generally less than 1 micrometer, are preferred for low-power and compact applications such as mobile devices and IoT technology, enabling enhanced performance while reducing power consumption and device size.</p></p>
