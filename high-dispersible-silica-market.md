<p><p>The high dispersible silica market comprises two main types: granular and micropearl. Granular high dispersible silica is characterized by larger particle sizes, offering superior flowability and handling properties, making it suitable for various applications like rubber and adhesives. Micropearl silica, on the other hand, consists of smaller, uniform particles that enhance dispersion and provide improved texture and surface finish in products such as cosmetics and coatings. Both types are essential for enhancing performance in diverse industries.</p></p>
