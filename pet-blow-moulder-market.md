<p><p>A PET blow moulder is a manufacturing machine designed to produce containers and bottles from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) resin through a blow molding process. The versatility and lightweight nature of PET make it a popular choice for industries such as beverages, personal care, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The PET blow moulder market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable packaging solutions and the rising consumption of bottled beverages.
