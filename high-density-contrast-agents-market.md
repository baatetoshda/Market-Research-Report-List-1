<p><p>High-density contrast agents are specialized substances used to enhance imaging in X-ray, CT, MRI, and ultrasound procedures. For MRI, high-density agents enhance signal contrast, allowing better differentiation of tissues. In ultrasound, they enhance echogenicity, improving the visibility of structures within the body. These contrast agents are essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning across various imaging modalities.</p></p>
